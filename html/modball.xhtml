Modball Rally 2019

The Background

One day, I was searching for a video editor job on the Internet. I found a man who wanted to hire an editor to edit daily vlogs of his Modball Rally adventure. He had to choose from 3 editors, so he decided to give some footage to all editors to edit them into a video as a test. Luckily, he chose me as his video editor.
Once he went on his journey, he recorded a lot of footage every day, uploaded it to Google Drive and I had a day to edit the whole thing. I was sitting in front of the computer for 2 weeks, but it was fun watching him and his friend traveling from Switzerland to London to Ibiza and back, even if I did not understand anything because I cannot speak French.


The Movie - Description

Modball Rally is a road trip organised each year for people who would like to visit the European countries by car, in a huge group. You have to register on their official website, you have the chance to bring anyone with yourself, your friends, family members, but there is a registration fee, which is around 3,000 - 5,000€ per head. Participants are accomodated in the best hotels, where they can discover the national cuisine, go to parties at night, or just simply relax and enjoy the view from their balconies.

This movie shows Pierre-Edouard Runser's and Régis Röbig's epic journey.

"What an epic adventure. The crazy Modball Rally 2019 going through London - Paris - Geneva - Barcelona - Circuit de Catalunya, Valencia, Ibiza and an accident at the end. Follow our funny stories, our new friends, our stunts, the parties, and the incident on the way back." - Pierre-Edouard Runser
